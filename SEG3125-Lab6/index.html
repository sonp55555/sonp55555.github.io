About Us


Paws Clinic is proud to be serving the Toronto area since 2001. Our team is committed to provide our clients, your pets, with a clean and friendly environment where we can treat them with loving care for every service. At our clinic, we treat your furry little friends like ours.




Our Services


It is essential that you have your pet's teeth checked at least annually as just like humans, dogs and cats also need their teeth cleaned professionally.

We offer professional grooming for all breeds of dogs and cats at our clinic.

If you are concerned that something may be wrong with your pet, please book an appointment for a medical consult. Depending on the symptoms, we may ask you to bring in your pet right away.

To ensure a proper diagnosis, we will need to thoroughly examine your pet.

Bringing your pet in for an annual wellness checkup can help reassure you that your dog or cat is healthy or help us detect hidden diseases or conditions early.
